How to Read SRC Format

Think of it like a recipe with clear ingredients and steps:

god.action tells you who is performing an action

motivation: explains why God took this action

gift: shows what God gave

purpose: reveals the intended outcome

The structure makes relationships clearer and helps you see patterns you might miss in traditional text.
